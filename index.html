DIMENSION

Dimension Project

Dimension Chain Project aims to build a bottom level decentralized data system based on blockchain technology. Dimension Chain records a subspace of the Internet upon request and rewards the effort of individual nodes. Empowered by Super Partners, Dimension Chain is capable of processing high data throughput, providing secured, faithful and permanent data storage and highly scalable to fulfill additional requests. Dimension Chain requires a minimum of energy input, in addition to the currently available internet structure. With Dimension Chain, we envision people’s future interaction with data.

FEATURE

Feature

Permanent Storage

Dimension Chain uses a distributed data storage system. The system includes numerous ordinary users each of who stores a shard of the whole database. A higher-level assurance to prevent data failure is achieved by employ Super Partners who hold a copy of the entire data.

Decentrallized Credibility

Dimension Chain collects data using a decentralized crawler-like mechanic. This mechanic ensures bias-free automatic data collection. The cost to record false information, or to temper exist date will be extremely high.

Authenticity verification

Dimension Chain collects data according to the smart contract published by users. The targeted data is filtered from the internet and forms a subspace. Blockchain system’s nature guarantees the authenticity of data to be constantly verified.

Highlight

Highlight

  • Hands-on

    The structure of Dimension Chain is optimized for data management. Users can modify the requests to best fit the needs for describing real events.

  • Efficient

    Minimum additional costs than current existing internet structure will be required for the operation of Dimension Chain. Super Partners work as the fundamental support of the whole structure. This system avoids the needs of various device regularly connect to the network.

  • Reliable

    Data has never been faithful and dependable as it will be on Dimension Chain. Blockchain technology naturally ensures the data to be accurate and complete.

  • Accessible

    Ordinary users can focus more on just their data and applications, instead of being distracted by the burden of heavy data processing duty.

FUTURE

Future Version

Cyber identity or "digital fingerprint" of any entity.

The blockchain system is based on a consensus agreement of all participants. All the information in a specific area about an entity becomes a proof of identity.

Future credibility or a new form of contracts.

The blockchain also allows users to announce any promise to be fulfilled in the up coming period. Backed by the system, this promise becomes a contract and will be executed without conditions.

A new way of collaboration. companies may become unnecessary for large projects.

As blockchain is commonly used to store all the information of daily activities, the data tends to be sufficient to organize personnel and resource effectively. Even large projects can be arranged merely on the base of tasks and events, instead of a traditional long-term contract.

APPLICATION

application

  • Influence measurement

    Dimension Chain’s capability to process high volume of data flow makes possible to capture all the user activities under a defined condition in a specific community. A project like this can easily measure how each individual participant’s opinion impacts on the whole community collectively. This could be a whole new perspective to see how mass media works.

  • Transport management

    Dimension Chain can provide a secured environment which allows each data transaction to use public or private key, or both. Using blockchain technology, along with other AI technologies, it is possible to build a transport or shipping network highly intelligent. The functions can be realized distributed decision making and behavior differentiation.

  • Personnel and Human Resource

    As more people use blockchain to record and describe real events, the community will gradually take the credibility into consideration. After people accept the credit of blockchain, the absolute credit backed by blockchain system will become the standard in certain areas. This will apply on work groups or education programs. Blockchain records will be one of, if not the only, accepted proof.
